Overview

uihash Summarizer ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users quickly obtain AI-powered summaries of webpage content. We value your privacy and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data We Collect

  1. Page Content - When you click the "Summarize" button, we extract text content from the current page. This data is only used to generate AI summaries and is not permanently stored on our servers.
  2. Account Information - If you choose to log in, we collect basic account info (nickname, avatar). We do not store your third-party login passwords.
  3. Usage Data - Summary count statistics, favorited articles and summaries, Todo items.
  4. Local Storage - User preferences, floating button position, chat history (stored locally in your browser).
What We Do NOT Collect:
  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not automatically scrape any page content in the background
  • We do not collect personal identity information (unless you log in)

How We Use Information

We use collected information only for:

  1. Core Service - Generate AI summaries of webpage content
  2. Account Management - Manage your login status and usage quota
  3. Service Improvement - Analyze usage patterns to improve product experience
  4. Customer Support - Respond to your questions and feedback

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

Third-Party Services

This extension uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Service Providers - For generating content summaries (data transmission encrypted, not permanently stored)
  • OAuth Login Providers - GitHub, Google, WeChat (only basic account info obtained)

Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  1. All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  2. Server data is encrypted at rest
  3. Regular security audits
  4. Principle of least privilege

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access - View information we collect about you
  2. Correct - Update your account information
  3. Delete - Request deletion of your account and related data
  4. Export - Export your favorites and Todo data
  5. Opt-out - Stop using the extension at any time

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
